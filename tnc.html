2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We collect location data only in the following scenarios:

  • When you press the SOS button

  • For discovering nearby volunteers and events
  • For providing personalized local content and community posts in your feed
  • For showing you relevant posts from your geographical area
  • For providing location-based services and recommendations

2.3 Usage Information

3.1 Core Functionality

  • User authentication and verification
  • Emergency response coordination

  • Local event discovery and recommendations
  • Service improvement and optimization
  • Research and development of new features
  • Analytics and performance monitoring

3.2 Emergency Contact System

  • Emergency contacts must explicitly accept their role
  • During emergencies, we share:

10.2 Content Posting and Limitations

To maintain quality and prevent misuse, we implement the following limitations:

  • Users are subject to daily post limits based on their account type
  • Standard accounts may post up to 5 times per day (subject to change)
  • Ambassador accounts may receive additional posting privileges
  • Daily interaction limits apply to likes and other engagement features
  • Users receive a daily allocation of 50 likes to encourage meaningful engagement (subject to change)
  • Post visibility is tied to user-specified locations (volunteering location or current location)
  • Users must consent to location sharing when posting content

All content must comply with our community standards:

  • Prohibited content includes but is not limited to:

  • Violations may result in content removal, account suspension, or termination
  • Users may appeal content removals through our designated process

10.4 Local Feed and Content Distribution

Content distribution is primarily location-based:

  • Content is primarily distributed based on geographic proximity
  • Posts appear in feeds of users within a relevant geographic radius
  • Your feed is personalized based on your current or selected location to show relevant community posts
  • Location data helps us connect you with nearby community members and their posts
  • The visibility radius may vary based on content type and user settings
  • Algorithm factors include:
    • Geographic distance
    • Content freshness
    • User engagement patterns
    • Account type and standing
    • Relevance to your local community
    • Location-based engagement trends

10.5 Account Types and Privileges

  • Standard accounts have base-level posting and engagement limits
  • Ambassador accounts may receive:
    • Increased daily post limits
    • Extended content visibility

  • Posts will be distributed based on our location-based algorithm as described in section 10.4
  • Users should exercise caution and careful judgment when posting content as it cannot be restricted to specific users

  • By posting content, you acknowledge and accept that it will be publicly accessible to all SOS Bharat users
